Academic Interpretation ‚Äì Exa 7.6B (Independent Samples t-Test)

The independent-samples t-test showed no significant difference in variance between the diets (p = 0.540), allowing the equal-variance approach. These results provide strong evidence that Diet A is more effective, resulting in an average additional loss of approximately 1.63 kg.
