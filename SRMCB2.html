This formative discussion will last for 3 weeks covering units 7, 8, and 9. Ensure you include appropriate citations and references in all your posts (this is not included in the word count for each week's post/submission).

Discussion Topic

Read Spring et al (2021) and then answer the following questions:

  • What characteristics of CVSS do the authors criticise? Do you agree with the critique? Justify your answer with academic references.
  • The authors also discuss a number of alternatives to CVSS. Select one of these alternatives and post an argument for why it should replace CVSS.

Instructions

You should demonstrate that you understand the topic covered and ensure you use references to academic literature (journals, books, reports, etc.). This activity will provide evidence of your personal growth. It will be a component of the e-portfolio, which you can submit at the end of the module for feedback.
