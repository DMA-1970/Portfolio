Seminar:

Information Technology Service Management Frameworks and Standards

📋 Seminar Preparation
  • Seminar Question: How can organisations leverage ITIL and COBIT frameworks to enhance IT service management practices and align them with business objectives effectively?
  • Familiarise yourself with the key components and principles of ITIL and COBIT frameworks, including their origins, evolutions, and best practices.
  • Review case studies or real-world examples of organisations that have successfully implemented ITIL and COBIT frameworks to optimise ITSM practices.
  • Prepare to participate in comparative analysis discussions, exploring the strengths and weaknesses of different ITSM frameworks and their applicability to diverse organisational contexts.
  • Seminar Tasks:
    • Considering the diverse range of IT Service Management (ITSM) frameworks available, such as ITIL, COBIT, and others, discuss the factors that organizations should consider when selecting a framework for implementation. How might organizational goals, industry regulations, and the complexity of IT infrastructure influence the choice of an ITSM framework? Provide examples to illustrate your points.
    • Regulatory compliance is a critical aspect of IT Service Management (ITSM), particularly in industries with stringent data privacy and security requirements. Discuss the challenges organizations may face when striving to achieve compliance with ITSM standards such as ISO/IEC 20000. How can organizations effectively address these challenges and ensure ongoing compliance with regulatory requirements while maintaining operational efficiency and service quality?
