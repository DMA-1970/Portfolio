Seminar: Programming Exercises

Preparation for this week’s seminar:
If you have never tried programming before, attempt the exercises in chapters 1 - 3 of Downey et al (2020). If you are a more experienced developer, attempt the exercises in chapters 4 and 5. Be prepared to demonstrate your solutions in this week’s seminar.

Ensure you attend or watch the recording of this week's seminar, as your tutor will be demonstrating a sort algorithm in two different languages as preparation for your Individual Programming Exercise, due in Unit 7.

Also this week, explore the likes of Python and C in more detail by making use of the following resources:

  • Python Tutorial (w3schools.com)
  • C Tutorial (w3schools.com)
  • Use Jupyter Notebook to write and run Python scripts. Download the latest version of Anaconda.
