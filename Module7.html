Weekly Reflection ‚Äì Unit 6

This week revisited descriptive statistics, including mean, median, mode, range, variance, and standard deviation. Although these concepts felt familiar from early education, I realised I had previously relied on them mechanically rather than analytically. Working through the workbook exposed how outliers distort means and how variation reveals hidden inconsistencies, challenging my assumptions about "average" performance. In my professional role, I often use these measures in reporting, but this unit forced me to question my interpretations more critically. Going forward, I will apply both central tendency and spread before drawing conclusions, strengthening the credibility of my operational analysis and capstone reporting.

Going forward, I will justify all visual choices and acknowledge their limitations.
