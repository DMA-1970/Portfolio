Applying the PEER method reinforced the need for evidence-based reasoning and clear linkage between findings and professional relevance.

Application: The learning underscored the value of structured design in research and enterprise practice. Frameworks such as CRISP-DM (IBM, n.d.) and Saunders et al.'s "research onion" guide transparency and systematic analysis. I will integrate quantitative and qualitative methods to evaluate AI governance and FinTech risk, enhancing both reliability and practical impact.

Forward Planning: I plan a mixed-method pilot study combining a short survey and targeted interviews to refine my data collection and analysis. Using the PEER structure, I will justify each choice and consult Sage Research Methods (2021) and QuestionPro (2021) to ensure rigour and relevance in my final MSc project.
